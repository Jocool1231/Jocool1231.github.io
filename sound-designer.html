SOUND DESIGNER

As a sound designer with over a decade of creative exploration, I've dedicated my career to transforming interactive experiences through meticulously crafted audio.

My expertise spans advanced sound design techniques, music composition, and audio implementation across multiple game engines, with a track record of breathing life into projects through innovative soundscapes.

From indie game jams to professional development, I've contributed audio to diverse titles like BIONICLE: Masks of Power, RADIOTOMB, and multiple Mech Encounter projects, consistently pushing the boundaries of interactive sound design.

My technical proficiency with tools like FMOD, WWise, and FL Studio, combined with a passion for narrative-driven audio, enables me to create immersive sonic experiences that resonate deeply with players.
