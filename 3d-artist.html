Masks of Power: Legacy was my first video game project, it is the precursor to what eventually became Bionicle: Masks of Power.

But do not be mistaken, this is a fully complete and well polished 5-8 hour long action RPG.

Built using Unreal Engine 4, it features a dynamic open-world environment, complex character mechanics, and interactive elements that enhance player immersion. I designed and implemented core gameplay systems, such as combat mechanics, environmental interactions, and progression tied to collectible masks, which grant unique abilities. The project involved creating custom assets, optimizing performance for seamless gameplay, and integrating sound and visual effects to enrich the player experience.

This game demonstrates my proficiency in problem-solving, level design, and technical optimization while adhering to a strong creative vision inspired by the Bionicle franchise.

I developed Crimson Veil as a first-person adventure game that showcases my expertise in interactive storytelling and gameplay mechanics. The game features a complex branching dialogue system, allowing players to shape their narrative experience through meaningful choices. I implemented a "Clues" system to encourage exploration and deduction, enhancing player engagement by tying story progression to discovered information.

Additionally, Crimson Veil includes a few puzzles that will challenge players' problem-solving skills, designed to integrate seamlessly with the game's narrative and environment. Built using Unity over the course of one month, the project highlights my ability to balance technical implementation with creative storytelling, and delivering an immersive and polished player experience, all in a timely manor.
