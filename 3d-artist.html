3D ARTIST

As a 3D artist and animator with over a decade of creative passion, I've dedicated my career to bringing imaginative worlds and characters to life through visual storytelling.

My expertise spans 3D modeling, animation, texturing, and rendering across multiple platforms, with a proven track record of creating compelling visual experiences for games, short films, and interactive media.

From the intricate animated series like The Bohrok Saga to game projects such as BIONICLE: Masks of Power, I've demonstrated versatility in crafting everything from character animations to complex environmental assets.

Proficient in Blender, Adobe Suite, and game engines like Unreal and Unity, I transform conceptual ideas into vibrant, dynamic visual narratives that capture imagination and drive engagement.

I had the incredible opportunity to create Ide's Journey: Freedom From Destiny, a 3D animated short film based on the world of Lego Bionicle, that tells a story of self-discovery and liberation.

Collaborating on this project, I contributed to animation, 3D modeling, sound editing, writing, and composing the original music score.

This endeavor allowed me to showcase my skills in storytelling, visual design, and audio production, all while refining my skills with Blender's powerful 3D toolset.

Bringing this vision to life and seeing it connect with audiences on YouTube was a very fulfilling experience.
