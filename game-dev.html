GAME DEVELOPER

I have been building my skills as a game developer for over a decade, through creating immersive game experiences and interactive stories. I am proficient in C#, C++, Unreal Engine, and Unity, I specialize in Gameplay Programming, UI Design/Implementation, Cinematics, and AI systems.

As the founder of Team Kanohi, I have led the development of BIONICLE: Masks of Power, showcasing my expertise in project management and technical execution.

My diverse portfolio spanning action RPGs, VR experiences, and game jam projects, should speak for itself. In my dedication, creativity, and work ethic.
